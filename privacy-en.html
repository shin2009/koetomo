Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

KoeOkosi ("the Service") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we handle data in the Service.

1. Audio Data

The Service uses your browser's speech recognition feature (Web Speech API) to convert speech to text.

  • Audio data is not sent to or stored on our servers
  • Speech recognition is processed by your browser's provider (e.g., Google for Chrome), and their privacy policy applies to that processing
  • Transcribed text (conversation logs) is stored only on your device (in localStorage) and is never transmitted externally

2. Translation Feature

When you use the real-time translation feature, recognized text is sent to the Google Translate API (translate.googleapis.com). Google's privacy policy applies to data sent through this service.

3. Analytics (Google Analytics)

The Service uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand usage patterns and improve the service. GA4 collects the following information:

  • Page view data (pages visited, time spent)
  • Device information (browser type, OS, screen size)
  • Approximate location (country/city level)
  • In-app actions (starting/stopping speech recognition, language switching, etc.)

This information is collected using cookies and browser identification technologies but does not include personally identifiable information. For details, see Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Information We Do Not Collect

The Service does not collect:

  • Personal information such as names or email addresses
  • Audio data itself
  • Contents of conversation logs

5. Data Storage

Conversation logs are stored only in your browser's localStorage on your device. Clearing your browser data will delete all conversation logs. No data is stored on our servers.

6. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

7. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as the Service evolves. Significant changes will be announced within the Service.

8. Contact

For questions about this policy, please contact the KoeOkosi development team.
